											<p>The Dalton Police Department compiles statistics on criminal activity and traffic crash and enforcement data periodically throughout the year.</p>
<p>Crime data is broken down into Part I (the most serious crimes) and Part II (other crimes) in accordance with the FBI&#8217;s Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) format. Each monthly report compares that month&#8217;s crime data to the same month in the previous year, and also compiles year-to-date data and compares it to the previous year. The traffic data included below is a monthly statistical report compiled by the Traffic Enforcement Unit (TEU) of crashes in Dalton highlighting roadways experiencing the most crashes and examines different contributing factors causing crashes in Dalton.</p>
